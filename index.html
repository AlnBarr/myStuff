Welcome to Own the Grey.


What is this place?

Hi, you have arrived here before we are ready. This is a placeholder for the Own The Grey website. In time we aim to develop this into a hub for people who have decided to stop fighting the signs of ageing and to “Own the Grey”. I believe that Older People have a lot to offer and are capable of learning new skills like HTML and CSS, perhaps even JavaScript. If I am right I will begin to expand this page into a much fuller site with resources and encouragement for those who visit. It may take months or even years, as I have a full-time job, at least for the next few years until retirement arrives. Perhaps you will find your way back to this page in a little while and it will have transformed into something more interesting. In the meanwhile why not click this link and go on an adventure across the internet.

Your adventure starts here!
